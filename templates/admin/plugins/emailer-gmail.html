Emailer (Gmail)

Gmail is a programmable email platform. It allows your application to become a fully featured email server. Send, receive and track messages with ease using your favorite programming language.

Imagination is your limit. Email is not hard anymore.

To get started:

  1. Register for an account on http://gmail.com. Gmail offers a free tier with up to 10,000 free emails monthly.
  2. (Optional) Set up a custom domain at https://gmail.com/cp/domains/new
  3. Paste your API key (not your public key) into the field below, hit save, and restart your NodeBB
